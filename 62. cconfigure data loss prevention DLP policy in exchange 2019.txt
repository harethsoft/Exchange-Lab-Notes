62. How to Configure Data Loss Prevention DLP Policy in Exchange 2019

Using Exchange Admin Center

Click on Compliance Management
Then click on Data Loss Prevention

Click the + icon
Select New custom DLP policy

Provide a name for the policy
Example
Block Aadhaar card and PAN information from being leaked

In Choose the state of this DLP policy
Select Enabled

In Choose the mode for the requirements in this DLP policy
Select Enforce

Click Save

Now select the newly created DLP policy
Click on the Edit icon

Go to the Rules tab
Click the + icon
Select Block messages with sensitive information

Configure the rule as required
Define the sensitive information types according to your requirement
For example select Aadhaar number and PAN number as the sensitive information types

Adjust the conditions and actions as needed based on the policy design

Click Save

This DLP policy will help prevent sensitive information such as Aadhaar and PAN details from being shared through email in Exchange 2019
