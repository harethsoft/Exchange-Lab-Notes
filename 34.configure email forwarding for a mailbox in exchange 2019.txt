

## 34. Configure Email Forwarding for a Mailbox in Exchange Server 2019

---

### Using Exchange Admin Center

1. Open **Exchange Admin Center**
2. Go to **Recipients**
3. Click **Mailboxes**
4. Select the mailbox
5. Click **Edit** (??)
6. Go to **Mailbox features**
7. Click **View details** under **Mail Flow**
8. Enable **Enable forwarding**
9. Select the recipient mailbox
10. (Optional) Enable **Deliver message to both forwarding address and mailbox**
11. Click **Save**

### Using Exchange Management Shell

Enable forwarding to another mailbox
Set-Mailbox user01 -ForwardingAddress saleh@harethsoft.lan -DeliverToMailboxAndForward $true



### Verify Forwarding Settings

Get-Mailbox user01 | fl ForwardingAddress,ForwardingSmtpAddress,DeliverToMailboxAndForward
