14. Configure Email Address Policy in Exchange 2019

Using Exchange Admin Center

Open Exchange Admin Center
Click on Mail Flow
Click on Email Address Policies tab
Click + to create a new policy
Provide a name for the policy
Specify the conditions for recipients the policy will apply to
Define the email address format (for example: [firstname.lastname@domain.com](mailto:firstname.lastname@domain.com))
Select which address should be primary
Click Save
Apply the policy to update recipients

---

Using Exchange Management Shell
New-EmailAddressPolicy -Name "DefaultPolicy" -IncludedRecipients AllRecipients -EnabledPrimarySMTPAddressTemplate "%g.%s@harethsoft2.com"


Update-EmailAddressPolicy "DefaultPolicy"

Get-EmailAddressPolicy | fl Name,EnabledPrimarySMTPAddressTemplate,IncludedRecipients
