12. Create Mail-Enabled Contact in Exchange 2019

Using Exchange Admin Center

Open Exchange Admin Center
Click on Recipients
Click on Contacts
Click +
Select Mail contact
Enter Name, External email address, Alias
Click Save

Using Exchange Management Shell

New-MailContact -Name "John Doe" -ExternalEmailAddress "johndoe@gmail.com" -Alias "johndoe"


Get-MailContact johndoe | fl Name,Alias,ExternalEmailAddress
