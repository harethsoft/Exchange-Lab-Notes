12. Create Mail-Enabled Contact in Exchange 2019

Using Exchange Admin Center

Open Exchange Admin Center
Click on Recipients
Click on Contacts
Click +
Select Mail contact
Enter Name, External email address, Alias
Click Save

Using Exchange Management Shell

New-MailContact `
-FirstName "Khaled" `
-LastName "Ali" `
-Name "Khaled Ali" `
-DisplayName "Khaled Ali" `
-Alias "khaled.ali" `
-ExternalEmailAddress "khaled.ali@gmail.com" `
-OrganizationalUnit "harethsoft.lan/Maddina/contacts"


Get-MailContact khaled.ali | fl Name,Alias,ExternalEmailAddress
