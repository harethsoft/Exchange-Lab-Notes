

Configure Message Moderation for a Group in Exchange

Click on Recipients section
Then click on Groups
Select the required group for example Surat Users
Click on the Edit icon

In the group properties page
Click on Message Approval

Select the option Messages sent to this group have to be approved by a moderator

Click the + icon to add a group moderator for example mohamed.ali

You can also specify users who do not require message approval

Click Save
