
8. Create Distribution Group in Exchange 2019

Using Exchange Admin Center

Open Exchange Admin Center
Click on Recipients
Click on Groups
Click on + (Add)
Select Distribution group
Enter the group name and alias
Add members to the group
Click Save

Using Exchange Management Shell

```
New-DistributionGroup `
-Name "hrusers3" `
-Alias "hrusers3" `
-Type Distribution `
-OrganizationalUnit "harethsoft.lan/maddina/hr" `
-ManagedBy "administrator@harethsoft.lan" `
-MemberJoinRestriction Closed `
-MemberDepartRestriction Closed

Add-DistributionGroupMember -Identity "hrusers3" -Member "hr1@harethsoft.lan"
Add-DistributionGroupMember -Identity "hrusers3" -Member "hr2@harethsoft.lan"
```
Verify the group

Get-DistributionGroup "hrusers3" | fl Name,Alias,Members
