

## 36. Add or Remove Email Addresses for a Mailbox in Exchange Server 2019

---

### Using Exchange Admin Center

1. Open **Exchange Admin Center**
2. Go to **Recipients**
3. Click **Mailboxes**
4. Select the mailbox
5. Click **Edit** (??)
6. Go to **Email address**
7. Click **+** to add new email address
8. Select **SMTP**
9. Enter the new email address
10. Click **Save**

Remove email address

1. Select the email address
2. Click **-**
3. Click **Save**

Set primary email address

1. Select the email address
2. Click **Set as reply address**
3. Click **Save**

---

### Using Exchange Management Shell

Add new SMTP email address
Set-Mailbox exadmin -EmailAddresses @{add="administrator@harethsoft.com"}

Remove SMTP email address
Set-Mailbox exadmin -EmailAddresses @{remove="administrator@harethsoft.com"}

Set primary SMTP address
Set-Mailbox exadmin -PrimarySmtpAddress [exadmin@harethsoft.local]

---

### Verify Email Addresses

Get-Mailbox exadmin| fl EmailAddresses,PrimarySmtpAddress
