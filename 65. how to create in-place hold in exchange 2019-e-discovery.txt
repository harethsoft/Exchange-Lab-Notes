## In-Place Hold & eDiscovery in Exchange 2019

---

## What is In-Place Hold?

It **freezes** a mailbox so emails cannot be deleted or modified, even by the user.

```
Normal mailbox = user can delete emails
Mailbox on Hold = emails are preserved forever ✅
```

---

## What is eDiscovery?

It allows you to **search** through mailboxes to find specific emails for legal or compliance purposes.

```
Example:
HR needs to find all emails between two employees
Legal team needs emails from last year about a specific topic
```

---

## When do you use them?

```
Legal investigation
Employee misconduct
Court order
Compliance audit
```

---

## How it works

```
1. Admin puts mailbox on Hold
2. User cannot delete any email
3. Admin searches mailbox using eDiscovery
4. Admin exports results as evidence
```

---

## Where to find it in Exchange

```
Exchange Admin Center
> Compliance Management
> In-Place eDiscovery & Hold
> Click + to create new
```

---

## Key points

```
Hold   = Preserve emails, prevent deletion
Search = Find specific emails by keyword, date, sender
Export = Download results for legal use
```
