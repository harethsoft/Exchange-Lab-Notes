61. How to Track Messages with Delivery Reports in Exchange 2019

Send a test email from [jay.shah@harethsoft.com](mailto:jay.shah@harethsoft.com) to [ali.ali@harethsoft.com](mailto:ali.ali@harethsoft.com)

Using Exchange Admin Center

Click on Mail Flow section
Then click on Delivery Reports tab

In Mailbox to search
Click Browse and select the mailbox ali.ali

In Search for messages received from
Select the sender jay.shah

Click Search

Review the search results to see the delivery status and details of the message including whether it was delivered delayed or failed
