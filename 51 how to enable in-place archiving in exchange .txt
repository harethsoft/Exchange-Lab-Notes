
Enable In Place Archiving in Exchange

Click on Mailboxes tab
Select the mailbox for which you want to enable archiving for example mohamed.ali
On the right side of the page you will see In Place Archive listed as Disabled click Enable
Select the database that will store the archive
Click OK

The user can now use Outlook or Outlook on the Web

Open Google Chrome and log in to Outlook Web App
Sign in with mohamed.ali
You will see In Place Archive mohamed.ali
Create folders under the archive
To archive an email click on any email then right click and select Archive


and using powershell

Enable-Mailbox -Identity "ali ali" -ArchiveName aliarchive -ArchiveDatabase db04
Get-Mailbox -Identity "ali ali" | fl *archive*
