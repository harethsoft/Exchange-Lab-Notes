9. Create Dynamic Distribution Group in Exchange 2019

Using Exchange Admin Center

Open Exchange Admin Center
Click on Recipients
Click on Groups
Click on + (Add)
Select Dynamic Distribution Group
Enter the group name and alias
Specify recipient filter criteria (e.g., department, company, state)
Click Save

Using Exchange Management Shell



New-DynamicDistributionGroup `
-Name "MaddinaUsers2" `
-Alias "MaddinaUsers2" `
-OrganizationalUnit "harethsoft.lan/Maddina" `
-IncludedRecipients MailboxUsers `
-RecipientContainer "harethsoft.lan/Maddina"

Set-DynamicDistributionGroup `
-Identity "MaddinaUsers2" `
-ManagedBy "administrator@harethsoft.lan"


Verify the group

Get-DynamicDistributionGroup "MaddinaUsers" | fl Name,Alias,RecipientFilter
